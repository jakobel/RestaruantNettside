Our Staff


Jakob Elversøe Solberg

Chef

Bocuse d’or winner in 2021, and captain of the national team of chefs from 2019 to date. Has a passion for local food, as well as using the finest ingredients found in nature. Has worked at several of the well-known restaurants in Oslo and at an already young age built up a very good reputation in the restaurant industry..


Sigurd Østlie

Sous chef

Masterchef winner in 2018, and has been a judge in Masterchef from 2019 -2020. Was brought by Jakob Solberg to this restaurant for innovation and put a modern touch on the menu. Is seen as the "next big thing"..


Thomas Wie

Sommelier

Educated in France, and is now back in Norway. Expert on especially French wines, as well as Italian dessert wines. Worked as a trainee at the Royal Palace.
